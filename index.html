Who We Are

A Startup Built to Solve Small Business Finance Problems

CompliEdge is a unit of Bikash Choudhary HUF, founded in 2026 with a clear mission — to be the trusted financial and compliance backbone for small businesses, startups, and entrepreneurs across Siliguri and West Bengal.

We saw firsthand how small business owners spend hours navigating GST portals, chasing CA appointments, managing payroll manually, and worrying about compliance notices — all while trying to run their actual business. We started CompliEdge to change that.

From the day you register your business to the day you scale it — we are your single point of contact for every financial, statutory, and legal requirement. Transparent pricing. Zero jargon. Real expertise.
